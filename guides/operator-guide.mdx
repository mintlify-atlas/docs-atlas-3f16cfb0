<Note>
**For:** Workshop operators  
**Access:** Only see the "Mi Turno" (My Shift) screen  
**What Can You Do?** View your assigned orders, register your work, and materials you use
</Note>

## How to Log In to the System

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Browser">
    Open the browser and enter the address given by your supervisor (e.g., `http://192.168.1.10`)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Type your **email** and your **password**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Click **"Ingresar"** (Log In)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you forgot your password, ask the owner or supervisor to change it for you.
</Tip>

---

## Main Screen — "Mi Turno" (My Shift)

When you log in, you directly see the **"Mi Turno"** screen.

Here you see **only the work orders assigned to you**.

Each order card shows:

- **Code** of the work order (e.g., `OT-0042`)
- **Title** of the job
- **Client** it's for
- **Current Status** (colored badge)
- **Committed Delivery Date**
- **Action Buttons**

---

## Work Order Statuses and Colors

| Status | Color | Meaning |
|--------|-------|----------|
| PLANIFICADA | Blue | Work order assigned to you, you haven't started yet |
| PENDIENTE | Gray | Waiting for you to start |
| EN PROCESO | Green | You are working on it |
| PAUSADA | Red | You stopped it temporarily |
| FINALIZADA | Dark Green | Work completed |

---

## Step by Step: How Do I Work on a Work Order?

### ▶ Step 1 — Starting the Work Order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Work Order">
    Find the work order in your list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Status">
    Verify the status is **PLANIFICADA** or **PENDIENTE**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Start">
    Click the **▶ Iniciar** (Start) button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status Changes">
    The status changes to **EN PROCESO** (IN PROCESS) green ✅
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
You can only start one work order at a time. If you already have one in process, the supervisor can reorganize them.
</Warning>

---

### ⏸ Step 2 — Pausing (If You Need to Interrupt)

If you need to stop temporarily (lunch, lack of material, etc.):

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find In-Process Work Order">
    Locate the work order that is **EN PROCESO**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Pause">
    Click **⏸ Pausar** (Pause)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status Changes">
    Status changes to **PAUSADA** (PAUSED) red
  </Step>
</Steps>

**To resume** the paused work order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Paused Work Order">
    Find it in your list (PAUSADA status in red)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Resume">
    Click **▶ Reanudar** (Resume)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status Returns">
    Status returns to **EN PROCESO**
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### ✅ Step 3 — Finishing the Work Order

When you finish the work:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Finish">
    Click **✅ Finalizar** (Finish)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation Window">
    A confirmation window opens
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Closing Note (Optional)">
    You can add an optional **closing note** (e.g., "Finished according to specification")
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The system records the exact finish time. Make sure to finish when the work is **really** done.
</Note>

## Registering Consumed Materials

While the work order is **EN PROCESO**, you can register which materials you used:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Register Consumption Section">
    Find the **"Registrar consumo"** (Register Consumption) section inside the work order
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Material">
    Search for the material in the search box (e.g., "varilla", "pintura")
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Quantity">
    Enter the **quantity** used
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register">
    Click **"Registrar"** (Register)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
This is very important because it allows the supervisor and owner to know the real cost of the work.
</Tip>

---

## Adding Notes or Comments

You can leave notes about the work order at any time:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Comment Field">
    Find the **"Agregar novedad / comentario"** (Add Update / Comment) field
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Note">
    Write your note (e.g., "Material X was missing, informed supervisor")
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add">
    Click **"Agregar"** (Add)
  </Step>
</Steps>

These notes are recorded with your name and the exact date/time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Why don't any work orders appear in my list?">
  The supervisor hasn't assigned you work yet. Consult them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I see other operators' work orders?">
  No. You only see your assigned orders.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I made a mistake registering consumption?">
  Notify the supervisor so they can correct it from their screen.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does the session close automatically?">
  Yes, after 8 hours you must log in again.
</Accordion>
